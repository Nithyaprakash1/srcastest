About the Club

The Heritage Club of Sri Ramakrishna College of Arts and Science is a distinguished student forum committed to the preservation, promotion, and appreciation of India’s cultural wealth. Established with the vision of nurturing cultural consciousness, the club provides students with a unique platform to explore traditions, values, and indigenous knowledge systems while fostering a spirit of inclusivity and innovation.

The Club actively integrates heritage awareness into academic and extracurricular life through curated events, heritage walks, expert interactions, and student-led initiatives that emphasize the importance of safeguarding our collective legacy.

Vision

To be a center of excellence that inspires students to value, preserve, and propagate the diverse cultural and heritage traditions of India.

Mission

  • To cultivate a deep sense of cultural identity and pride among students.
  • To organize impactful programmes that highlight tangible and intangible heritage.
  • To provide a platform for creative expression rooted in cultural values.
  • To engage students in heritage conservation through experiential learning.
  • To foster leadership, unity, and responsibility in preserving traditions for future generations.

Core Activities

The Heritage Club undertakes initiatives that combine knowledge, creativity, and community engagement:

  • Orientation & Awareness Drives – instilling cultural consciousness among new members.
  • Workshops & Masterclasses – on traditional art forms, crafts, and literature.
  • Cultural Showcases – exhibitions, performances, and inter-collegiate competitions.
  • Heritage Walks & Field Visits – to historical sites, museums, and monuments.
  • Collaborative Projects – with cultural organizations, NGOs, and peer clubs.
  • Festivals & Observances – celebrating India’s diversity through thematic events.

Leadership & Governance

The Heritage Club is guided and supported by dedicated faculty members and student leaders who strive to uphold the club’s vision and mission. Together, they create a vibrant and sustainable platform that celebrates culture while empowering students to take pride in their heritage.
